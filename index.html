Design Research Is

a collection of techniques for gathering the appropriate information for a project and explores the nuances of implementing those techniques through case studies

Design research is the deliberate process of asking the right people the right questions to ensure that we are creating solutions with empathy for real users.

We conduct design research in order to solve for a lack of information and to answer questions. Research ensures our design choices and other decision-making are informed by qualitative data instead of assumptions.
